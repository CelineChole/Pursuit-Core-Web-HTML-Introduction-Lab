PB & J Recipe

Description

This is the recipe for a peanut butter and jelly sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 jar of some bomb grape jelly
  • 1 jar of peanut butter
  • A spoon or a knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With the butter knid=fe, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. On the rigt side, spread a 1/8 inch of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half.
  6. Enjoy!!
